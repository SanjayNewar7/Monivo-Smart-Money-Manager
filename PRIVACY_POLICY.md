## Introduction

Monivo: Smart Money Manager respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles user data.

## Information Collection

Monivo does **not collect or store personal financial data on external servers**.

All financial information entered in the app such as:

- Expense records
- Income records
- Account information
- Transaction history

is stored **locally on the user's device only**.

---

## How Your Data Is Used

The information stored in the app is used only to provide the following features:

- Tracking expenses and income
- Managing multiple accounts
- Displaying financial insights
- Organizing transaction history

No personal financial data is transmitted outside your device.
